Call for Papers

We welcome high-quality research articles that illustrate research results, projects, and review works in the following areas, but not limited to:

Topics of Interest

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Data Warehousing and Mining
  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics
  • Cloud, Edge and Fog Computing
  • Internet of Things and Cyber-Physical Systems
  • Cybersecurity and Blockchain Technologies
  • Quantum and High Performance Computing
  • Robotics and Automation
  • Digital Twin and Metaverse Technologies
  • Image Processing and Remote Sensing
