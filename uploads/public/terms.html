✍️ Author Responsibility

Authors submitting to the International Journal of Scientific Research in Computing (IJSRC) must ensure that:

  • Research is conducted ethically and follows institutional and legal guidelines.
  • Manuscripts are original and properly cited.
  • No instances of plagiarism or redundant publication occur.
  • The views and opinions in articles are those of the authors and do not necessarily reflect the policies of the journal or editorial board.

Failure to adhere to these responsibilities may result in manuscript rejection, retraction, or other corrective actions.
